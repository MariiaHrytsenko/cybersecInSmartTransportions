Cybersecurity in Transportation Infrastructure

1. Categories of Systems in Transportation Infrastructure

Transportation infrastructure relies on three main types of systems:

  • IT (Information Technology) ‚Äì information systems that process data (ticketing systems, corporate services, archives).
  • OT (Operational Technology) ‚Äì systems that control physical processes (train control automation, SCADA systems in airports).
  • CT (Communication Technology) ‚Äì networking technologies for system communication (GPS, satellite communication, V2X communication).

2. Vulnerabilities in Each Category

Category Key Threats
IT Phishing, database attacks, exploits on web services.
OT Physical threats, manipulation of control signals, remote intrusion in SCADA.
CT GPS spoofing, communication interception, DDoS attacks on infrastructure.

3. Real Cyber Incidents

GPS Jamming in Europe

  • Impact on Aviation: route deviations, navigation risks.
  • Impact on Logistics: disruption of road transport, navigation issues for maritime vessels.
  • Incidents: reported GPS outages in Scandinavia and the Baltic, likely caused by Russian interference.

Cyber Attack on Colonial Pipeline (2021)

  • Impact on OT: disruption of SCADA systems due to ransomware attack.
  • Business Consequences: fuel shortages on the East Coast of the USA, millions of dollars in damages.
  • Attack Analysis: exploitation of a vulnerability in VPN access, lack of network segmentation.
